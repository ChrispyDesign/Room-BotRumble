CONTROLS
Requires Xbox controllers and supports up to 4 players. 

You are always moving forward. Punches need to be wound up for a minimum time before a punch can be fired. s

Left Bumper: Rotate to the left, and wind up a punch. Release to fire the punch. 
Right Bumper: Rotatet to the right, and wind up a right punch. Release to fire the punch
Left Stick: Aim your left punch
Right Stick: Aim your right punch
